REVIEW OF RELATED LITERATURE
Foreign Literature
Digital governance improves transparency and citizen participation worldwide. AI and ICT tools help make communication clearer, decisions more data-driven, and services more accessible. Studies show digital platforms strengthen community cooperation and support local entrepreneurship. Literature emphasizes that successful digital systems must align technology, users, and organizational readiness.
Local Literature
Philippine barangays increasingly adopt digital tools but struggle with limited skills and infrastructure. Social media and basic online systems help speed up announcements and coordination. Research shows ICT training improves engagement and transparency, but most existing systems remain fragmented. Local literature highlights the need for integrated platforms that combine governance, communication, and livelihood support.
